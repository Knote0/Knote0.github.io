inFin: AI Voice Notes

The Ultimate AI Voice Notes App

Capture ideas and conversations effortlessly with inFin. Whether it's a fleeting thought, an important discussion, a detailed meeting, or a challenging foreign language lesson, inFin simplifies note-taking, converting voice to text, and translating in real time.

Features

Simple and Easy to Use

Designed for simplicity, inFin offers an intuitive, clutter-free experience. With minimal design and no annoying pop-ups, it is extremely user-friendly. Language selection only appears on first use, ensuring a seamless experience every time.

Unlimited Recording to Text

Start recording with a single click. inFin ensures HD audio quality, whether for short ideas or lengthy meetings. With no limits on recording, it captures everything in real time.

Unlimited Real-Time Translation

inFin supports unlimited transcription and real-time translation in both Chinese and English. It offers highly accurate transcription and simultaneous translation, making it perfect for foreign language courses or international meetings. It also formats text automatically for easy review and editing.

Independent of Network Environment

With cutting-edge offline recognition technology, inFin allows recording and translation without an internet connection, ensuring accuracy and convenience in any situation.

Local Storage, Security, and Privacy

All personal data is stored locally on your device. inFin guarantees no reading or selling of user data, ensuring privacy and security. Use inFin with confidence, knowing it is safer than native apps on your phone.
